// 01 — The Problem

Mobility is a right,
not a privilege.

Over 131 million people globally face mobility challenges. 65 million rely on wheelchairs daily — yet 75% of those are manual, requiring physical effort and constant help.

75% are on
manual chairs
⚡ Manual Wheelchairs
Affordable at ₹1,500–₹15,000, but demand significant physical effort. Users often can't operate independently.
💰 Electric Wheelchairs
Overcome physical barriers, but cost ₹45,000–₹3,00,000 — completely out of reach for most users.
🚫 The Gap
No affordable, smart, and reliable middle-ground exists. Until now.
// 02 — Our Solution

One kit.
Total transformation.

SmartDrive Assist is a modular add-on that snaps onto any existing manual wheelchair, converting it into a fully-featured smart electric wheelchair. No replacement needed.

₹10K
Target retail price
vs ₹45K–₹3L for electric chairs
// What it does
→ Converts manual to electric with a single toggle
→ Charges itself while being pushed (Dynamo Motor)
→ Detects obstacles & drop-off edges automatically
→ GPS tracking + SOS alerts for safety
→ Bluetooth remote + voice control
→ Fits any standard wheelchair — zero modifications
